## Overview

The Kit MCP server (formerly ConvertKit) connects AI assistants like Claude to the Kit.com email marketing platform, enabling management of email marketing workflows and audience engagement for creators.

## Features

- **Subscriber Management**: Add, update, and segment subscribers
- **Tag Management**: Efficiently handle subscriber tags and organization
- **Email Sequences**: Create and manage automated email sequences
- **Broadcast Creation**: Design and send email broadcasts
- **Analytics**: Analyze subscriber data and campaign performance

## Platform Integration

The ConvertKit MCP Server allows MCP-compatible clients to connect to ConvertKit and manage email marketing workflows through natural language interactions.

## Use Cases

- Automate subscriber onboarding and segmentation
- Manage email campaigns through conversational AI
- Analyze audience engagement and metrics
- Create and schedule email sequences
- Handle subscriber lifecycle management

## Target Audience

Designed for content creators, bloggers, podcasters, and online entrepreneurs who use email marketing to build and engage their audience.

## Benefits

Streamlines email marketing operations by enabling natural language management of complex workflows, reducing manual tasks and improving creator productivity.
