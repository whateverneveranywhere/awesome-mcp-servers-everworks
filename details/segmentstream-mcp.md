## Overview

SegmentStream MCP turns AI assistants into agentic marketing analysts by exposing nine core measurement technologies built over seven years for managing $100M+ ad spend.

## Features

- Identity Graph for cross-device user profiles
- Cross-Channel Attribution for accurate ROAS/CPA
- Self-Reported Reattribution for dark funnel channels
- Marketing Mix Optimization for automated budget reallocation
- Incrementality Testing with geo holdouts and synthetic controls
- Signal Quality with synthetic conversions via CAPI
- Visit Scoring with ML-based session assessment
- Predictive Lead Scoring and Customer LTV Prediction
- 30+ turnkey integrations (Google, Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.)
- 100+ pre-built skills for performance analysis, anomaly detection, reporting
- Data stored in user's BigQuery for no lock-in
- Shareable reports with live links and PDFs
